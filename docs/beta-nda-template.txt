## 1. What This Is

SoftBacon is giving Tester early access to **Mycelium** — a distributed AI agent coordination platform — as a private beta customer. In exchange, Tester agrees to keep certain information confidential and to provide honest feedback. This Agreement spells out the rules for that arrangement.

---

## 2. Confidential Information

**2.1 What's covered.** "Confidential Information" means anything non-public that SoftBacon shares with Tester in connection with the beta, including:

- Platform architecture, source code, and technical design
- Business plans, roadmap, pricing models, and go-to-market strategy
- Internal tooling, agent coordination protocols, and APIs
- Any other information SoftBacon marks as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the context

**2.2 What's not covered.** The following are not Confidential Information:

- Information Tester can show was already publicly known before receiving it
- Information Tester received from a third party without any confidentiality obligation
- Information Tester independently developed without reference to SoftBacon's materials
- Information SoftBacon later makes public

**2.3 Obligations.** Tester agrees to:

- Keep Confidential Information secret using at least the same care they use for their own sensitive information (and no less than reasonable care)
- Not share it with anyone else without SoftBacon's written permission
- Use it only for evaluating and using Mycelium under this Agreement

---

## 3. Beta Software — No Warranty

Tester understands and accepts that:

- Mycelium is **pre-release software**. It may contain bugs, incomplete features, or behave unexpectedly.
- SoftBacon makes **no warranties**, express or implied, about Mycelium's fitness for any particular purpose, reliability, or performance.
- SoftBacon is **not liable** for any damages arising from Tester's use of the beta — including but not limited to lost data, lost revenue, or business interruption.
- Features may change or be removed at any time during the beta period.

Use the beta with that understanding. Back up anything important.

---

## 4. Tester's Data

**4.1 Isolation.** Tester's Mycelium instance is logically isolated. Other users cannot access Tester's data.

**4.2 SoftBacon access.** SoftBacon may need to access Tester's instance data for support and debugging. SoftBacon will provide **reasonable advance notice** before accessing Tester's data, except in emergencies (e.g., a security incident or platform-wide outage) where access may happen immediately.

**4.3 No selling data.** SoftBacon will not sell, share, or use Tester's instance data for any purpose other than operating and improving the platform.

**4.4 On termination.** When the beta period ends, Tester may request an export of their data. SoftBacon will make reasonable efforts to provide it within 14 days of the request.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property

**5.1 SoftBacon owns Mycelium.** The Mycelium platform — including its architecture, code, protocols, and any improvements made during the beta — is and remains the sole property of SoftBacon Software. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any ownership of Mycelium to Tester.

**5.2 Tester owns what they build.** Anything Tester creates *using* Mycelium — projects, agents, workflows, applications — belongs to Tester. SoftBacon has no claim over Tester's work product.

**5.3 No competing platform.** Tester agrees not to use knowledge gained through access to Mycelium — including its architecture, design patterns, or internal mechanics — to build or substantially contribute to a directly competing platform (i.e., a distributed AI agent coordination or orchestration platform offered to third parties). This restriction does not prevent Tester from building their own web apps, tools, or products with AI agents.

---

## 6. Feedback

Tester agrees to provide honest feedback about their experience — what works, what doesn't, bugs encountered, and feature ideas. This can be informal (messages, notes, conversations).

Tester grants SoftBacon a perpetual, royalty-free license to use any feedback to improve Mycelium. SoftBacon is not obligated to credit Tester for feedback incorporated into the product, and incorporating feedback does not give Tester any ownership interest in Mycelium.

---

## 7. Term and Termination

**7.1 Duration.** This Agreement begins on the date signed and continues until the earlier of:

- SoftBacon's commercial launch of Mycelium (at which point Tester will be offered standard commercial terms), or
- Termination by either party with **30 days written notice** (email is sufficient)

**7.2 Early termination.** Either party may terminate immediately if the other materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 10 days of written notice.

**7.3 Survival.** Sections 2 (Confidential Information), 4 (Data), 5 (Intellectual Property), and the no-competing-platform restriction in 5.3 survive termination for **2 years**. Section 5.1 (SoftBacon IP ownership) survives indefinitely.

**7.4 On termination.** Tester will stop using the platform and, upon request, destroy or return any Confidential Information in their possession.
